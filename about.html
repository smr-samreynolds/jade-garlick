Get to know Jade Garlick

Meet Jade Garlick, a seasoned travel professional with over a decade of experience and a passion for exploration. Her journey began with an inspiring European adventure, leading her to defer her Bachelor of Policing to pursue her wanderlust wholeheartedly.

Joining the Flight Centre Travel Group in 2013, Jade's career soared alongside her adventures. Progressing through leadership roles, from opening a new store to venturing into the luxury domain with Travel Associates, Jade's dedication to excellence only intensified.

Driven by her love for travel and a commitment to client satisfaction, Jade envisioned a service like no other. Thus, Roast & Roam was born—a luxury agency dedicated to crafting bespoke travel experiences all while sipping on your favourite brew. Her ethos revolves around curating unforgettable moments tailored to each client's desires, filled with insider knowledge and thoughtful advice.

For Jade, it's about more than just booking a trip; it's about providing personalised journeys with surprises along the way. No travel brief is too big or small—she values the trust of each client, promising to be their personal superhero when they're far from home.

Why Roast & Roam?

Roast & Roam isn't just another travel agency; we're your dedicated partners in crafting extraordinary adventures. With a genuine passion for travel and a commitment to excellence, we go above and beyond to ensure the best for our clients. Say goodbye to the stress of planning and hello to a seamless journey from start to finish. Our mission is simple: to help you create memories that last a lifetime. Whether you're dreaming of sipping coffee on a bustling European street or trekking through remote wilderness, we're here to turn your travel dreams into reality. Let Roast & Roam be your guide as you embark on the journey of a lifetime.

Roast & Roam is proud to be an affiliate of Itravel, a leading name in the travel industry. This partnership allows us to offer our clients access to a vast network of resources, ensuring that every aspect of their journey is expertly taken care of. With the backing of Itravel's extensive experience and global reach, we can guarantee unparalleled service and support, from the moment you start planning to the moment you return home. Together, Roast & Roam and Itravel are committed to providing you with unforgettable travel experiences that exceed your expectations.
