Our Services

When you entrust your travel plans to Roast and Roam, you are benefiting from the expertise of a seasoned and trusted travel advisor who wants what is best for you.

Roast & Roam accompanies you through every stage of your journey, beginning with your initial planning session, booking, offering unique recommendations and advice tailored just for you, we are there for you before you travel with any last minute questions, while you travel should you encounter any troubles and when you safely return home. Our meticulous attention to detail ensures a worry-free experience tailored perfectly to your needs.

Our service fees encompass our comprehensive Roaming Experience, ensuring that every dream or bucket list item you have receives the dedicated care, time and personalisation it deserves.

These fees cover various aspects of the booking process, from initial consultation and itinerary planning to actual booking, amendments, and cancellations. It's important to note that many of these fees are non-refundable, particularly the deposits and booking fees, so it's advisable to be certain of your travel plans before booking to avoid additional costs.
